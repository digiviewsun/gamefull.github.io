Digital marketing:

Earning process in online is not a smooth way.So a online earning person has to face new

challenge daily.All the way of online earning go its individual way .But only online publishing

is not the ending of online earning process.Product,digital product,person's work all have to be 

known by real human person all over the world.Unless it ,the online earning will not  reach the 

destination   and  make a unsuccessful project.


The process of marketing all product, digital product,person's work  via online is called 

digital marketing.   


Types of digital marketing:

  • By video promotion service
  • By social media promotion service
  • By seo service
  • By content promotion service
